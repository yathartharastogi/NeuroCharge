# PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS DOCUMENT (PRD)
**Project:** NeuroCharge - Brain-Inspired EV Battery Intelligence Platform

## 1. PRODUCT OVERVIEW
NeuroCharge is a neuromorphic battery intelligence platform that learns charging behavior, predicts degradation, detects thermal anomalies, and optimizes charging cycles using Spiking Neural Networks (SNNs).

## 2. PROBLEM STATEMENT
Current Battery Management Systems rely on static thresholds and rule-based logic. They cannot adapt to individual battery behavior, resulting in:
- Faster battery degradation
- Reduced driving range
- Inefficient charging patterns
- Increased maintenance costs
- Safety risks due to thermal events

## 3. OBJECTIVES
- Learn charging patterns in real time
- Predict battery degradation before failure
- Detect thermal anomalies instantly
- Recommend healthier charging strategies
- Extend battery lifespan

## 4. TARGET USERS
**Primary:**
- EV Owners

**Secondary:**
- Fleet Operators
- EV Manufacturers
- Battery Researchers

## 5. SUCCESS METRICS
- >90% degradation prediction accuracy
- <1 second anomaly detection latency
- 10-15% lifespan improvement
- <500ms dashboard response time

## 6. CORE FEATURES
**F1. Battery Health Monitoring**
- State of Health (SOH)
- State of Charge (SOC)
- Temperature
- Charge Cycles

**F2. Neuromorphic Learning Engine**
- Learns charging habits
- Learns thermal behavior
- Adapts to user-specific patterns

**F3. Degradation Prediction**
- Remaining Useful Life
- Future SOH estimation
- Capacity fade forecasting

**F4. Thermal Event Detection**
- Overheating detection
- Rapid temperature spike detection
- Unsafe charging behavior alerts

**F5. Smart Charging Recommendations**
- Optimal charging limits
- Charging schedule suggestions
- Battery preservation recommendations
